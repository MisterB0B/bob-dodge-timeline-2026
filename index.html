RFID + QR Code Dual System

Why RFID is critical: RFID tags allow truck loaders to scan entire pallets and racks instantly. Without RFID, loaders would have to manually scan 4-5 props on every pallet and unstack large metal coro racks to scan every individual QR code — a process that would take many hours and severely slow down the trucking operation. RFID enables us to know what's in every truck in a timely fashion, making load-out efficient and accurate.

QR codes complement RFID: QR codes are created for every prop and tie into our maintenance tracker system. On-site techs scan QR codes to record any maintenance or repairs done on props during the show season. When props return to the warehouse, we immediately know what needs to be worked on — no guesswork, no surprises.

Summary: RFID = fast truck loading/unloading (bulk scanning). QR codes = detailed maintenance tracking at venues. Both essential to operations.
