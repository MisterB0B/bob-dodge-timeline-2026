2026 Implementation Timeline

Technical & Creative Operations
Bob Dodge — Production Designer | The Light Park
Planning Horizon: February 2026 – January 2027

Executive Summary

This timeline organizes 12 new operational roles and existing responsibilities into 5 strategic workstreams across 4 quarterly phases. The plan sequences high-impact items first, leverages new hires to offload operational work, and batches related efforts to maximize efficiency.

Infrastructure & Systems

Shop Manager, inventory systems, xLights optimization

People & Knowledge

Training, Prop Bible, warehouse operations transition

Creative & Brand

Character development, new songs, content creation

Guest Experience

Tent design, merch displays, mobile app development

Strategic Expansion

Walk-through business planning

Phase 1: Foundation & Quick Wins

February – April 2026 Get systems online, hire key people, stop the bleeding

🛠️ Infrastructure & Systems

  • Deploy Shop Manager app company-wide
  • Onboard team to Shop Manager system
  • Begin Flex inventory software setup & configuration
  • Order truss for pipe tunnel props (more efficient build, better visual product for guests)
  • Audit current xLights layouts across all 8 venues
  • Begin xLights optimization: easier sequencing, faster render times, streamlined FPP deployment

👥 People & Knowledge Transfer

  • Finalize hiring — AutoCAD Draftsman & Shop Manager
  • Onboard draftsman with tools, standards, file structure
  • Prop Bible framework created — ready for draftsman to populate with technical drawings
  • Reallocate props to existing parks and lay out in themed areas (reference marketing deck)
  • Onboard shop manager with warehouse SOPs and expectations
  • Train shop technician on controller upload process (2000+ units)

🔧 Existing Responsibilities

  • Equipment maintenance — staff going through all inventory to ensure readiness for 2026 season (all venues)
  • Post-strike inventory reconciliation
  • Design work for 2026 season (8 venues)

Key Milestones

  • Shop Manager app live by end of Feb
  • Both new hires onboarded by end of Mar
  • Flex inventory software configured with initial data by end of Apr
  • Prop Bible framework complete — draftsman to populate first 10 entries by end of Apr

Phase 2: Build & Train

May – July 2026 Develop brand, train team, build systems

🛠️ Infrastructure & Systems

  • Complete Flex inventory data migration (all props, all venues)
  • Complete xLights optimization for all 8 venues (sequencing workflow, render performance, FPP deployment)
  • Update existing sequences for new optimized layouts
  • Integrate Flex with distribution workflow

👥 People & Knowledge Transfer

  • Draftsman produces prop drawings (Bob reviews, delegates)
  • Prop Bible — assembly/disassembly instructions for all major props
  • Shop manager independently running warehouse operations
  • Shop technician independently uploading sequences/programs
  • Create training documentation for controller programming

🎨 Creative & Brand

  • Define character universe bible (DJ Polar Ice, Pixel Penguin, Gnomies)
  • Commission 3 new songs for 2026 show
  • Create "bumper" tracks between songs for character dialog (bring personality to the show)
  • Send new songs for professional sequencing
  • Concept new character-driven sequences
  • Begin AI avatar development for characters
  • Design innovative prop concepts for 2026 characters
  • Beta test AI-driven sequencing software

🎪 Guest Experience & Technology

  • Design cohesive check-in tent environment (standard for all 8 parks)
  • Create new merch display prototype for tents
  • Plan concessions/merch showcase integration in check-in
  • Begin mobile app architecture & design (in-house)
  • Develop mobile app MVP — core guest engagement features
  • Research AR game frameworks and geo-located audio tech

Key Milestones

  • Full inventory digitized in Flex by end of Jun
  • All 8 venue xLights layouts optimized (sequencing, render times, FPP deployment) by end of Jul
  • Shop technician certified for independent controller uploads by end of Jun
  • Warehouse manager running daily ops independently by end of Jul
  • Character bible complete by end of May
  • AI sequencing software evaluated with initial results by end of Jul
  • Check-in tent design finalized and approved by end of Jun
  • Mobile app MVP (basic functionality) by end of Jul

Phase 3: Production & Integration

August – October 2026 Build season — everything comes together

🎨 Creative & Brand

  • Produce new character animations for shows
  • Finalize character voices and dialog tracks
  • Create new bumper tracks for all venues
  • Launch content creation campaigns (video, social, behind-the-scenes)

🎪 Guest Experience & Technology

  • Build check-in tent prototypes at first 2 venues
  • Finalize mobile app features for launch
  • Beta test mobile app with internal team
  • Prepare app for public launch

📈 Strategic Expansion

  • Research walk-through experience market
  • Develop proof-of-concept walk-through design
  • Cost estimate and feasibility study for walk-through expansion

🔧 Build Season Operations

  • Venue installations at all 8 sites
  • Quality control testing across all systems
  • Final technical rehearsals and adjustments

Key Milestones

  • New bumper tracks delivered for all venues by end of Sep
  • Check-in tents deployed at 2 pilot venues by end of Sep
  • Mobile app ready for public launch by end of Oct
  • Walk-through POC and feasibility study delivered by end of Oct

Phase 4: Launch, Operate & Refine

November 2026 – January 2027 Opening night, live operations, continuous improvement

🎪 Show Operations

  • Opening night across all 8 venues
  • Monitor guest feedback and app engagement
  • Real-time adjustments to shows based on feedback
  • Content creation during live season (behind-the-scenes, testimonials)

📊 Analysis & Planning

  • Track season metrics (attendance, app usage, merch sales)
  • Gather team feedback on new systems and workflows
  • Identify improvement opportunities for 2027

🔧 Post-Season Wrap-up

  • Lead strike team across all venues
  • Equipment maintenance — staff going through all inventory to ensure readiness for next season
  • Inventory reconciliation via Flex
  • Season retrospective — document lessons learned
  • Begin planning 2027 enhancements

Key Milestones

  • Successful 2026 season launch across 8 venues
  • Mobile app live for opening weekend
  • 15%+ of guests download/use app during first season
  • 2027 enhancement plan delivered by end of Jan
